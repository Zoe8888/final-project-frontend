Diary of the Hodophiles

Greetings and welcome to the virutal diary of Hodophiles from around the world!

You may be wondering, "What is a hodophile?"
The word orginates from the Greek langauge and is definied as a lover of travel.

This blog was created with the purpose of showing fellow travelers adventure's from all over the globe.
This is intended to be a safe space where we will be able to learn and explore through one anothers unique journies.
